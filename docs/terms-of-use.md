## 1. Service Description

Jana is an AI assistant application for the Frappe framework. Jana facilitates communication between you and third-party Large Language Model (LLM) providers to generate AI-powered responses, content suggestions, and automated actions within your Frappe site.

Jana does **not** operate its own AI models. All AI capabilities are provided by external LLM providers that you configure and connect.
